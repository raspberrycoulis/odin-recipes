Beef Chilli Recipe

A spicy beef chilli

This is a hearty beef chilli, packed with flavor and just the right amount of heat.

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Kidney beans
  • Chilli powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. In a large pot, brown the beef mince over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic, cooking until softened.
  3. Stir in the chopped tomatoes, kidney beans, and spices.
  4. Simmer for at least 30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
