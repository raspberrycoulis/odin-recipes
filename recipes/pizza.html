Pizza Recipe

A pepperoni pizza

This is a classic pizza, with mouth watering slices of pepperoni and the finest buffalo mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Buffalo mozzarella
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Top with slices of buffalo mozzarella and pepperoni.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh basil before slicing and serving.
